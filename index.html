About Me

:/

Hey there! This is a sketchy website I made just to provide a quick overview of fields that I have been working on over the years.


My projects are created from passion, and I love to combine my skills in various areas together, which gives me a clearer view on how I might be able to use my skills someday. I started learning 3D modeling, texturing and rendering seriously since middle school. As for music, I mainly enjoy writing lyrics and keep the majority of my songs unreleased since recording and producing has been a great challenge that I am still actively working on. Photography is also something I do in my free time.


While it is crucial for me to develop technical skills, I don't forget to enjoy what I am doing. I'm always interested in exploring unknown fields and learning new skills to help with me along my creative journey.

My Work

3D Concept Art

The entire 3D process is always made in 3D software Blender. For some of them, e.g. the artwork I am showcasing here, I also used Davinci Resolve for the final video for color grading purposes. This artwork is a practice on rendering a large amount of simple objects to create the illusion of complex details for massive heavy scenes, in this case it is the city. With a red and blue tone and the character standing at a high ground, I took inspirations from the Spider-Man movies

Product Design

What you see here is what I call a "lightclock", it is a part of my lamp design series. For this particular light source, as you can see, since there are 12 individual light bulbs around the ring, one of them being lit can be used to indicate time like a clock while lighting up the scene. I also designed other functionalities, for example, you can use it as a counter, counting up to 12 as the maximum. Colors and light intensity are also adjustable.

Music

Music is a great way for me to express my emotions and ideas. What I like to focus on is HipHop Music. By writing punchlines and doing wordplays, this creative process adds a new level of entertainment. I made an album titled "WATER" which was unreleased since I was not happy enough with the result, but I do love the first three songs on the tracklist - "UNDERWATER", "SOLO" and "WORTHY". It sort of shows this metaphor of me going from "drowning" to helping myself out.

Photography

Photography is a great way to represent the beauty of this world we live in. It is also one of the best methods to capture reality, Through the lenses of my camera, and sometimes my iPhone, I get to keep a lot of memories. I love capturing scenery no matter if it's natural or man-made. When spending time with friends and family, it is great getting to be the one who records these moments. I also enjoy the post-processing process of bringing photos to a whole new level primarily using Photoshop.

Abstract art

Despite seemingly being farily easy to create, abstract art actually requires a lot of brainstorming to satisfy the audience on an artistic level. Playing with modifiers and other functionalities of the software, shows how many possibilities you get to unlock inside the software when making abstract art. It is the art of pushing limits, and as a casual 3D artist, creating abstract art has definitely boosted my skills and forced me to step outside my comfort zone and make use of software features I am unfamiliar with.

Style Replication

This is where I study how video games implement their visual styles and try to recreate them in 3D. Although I have not played Minecraft since elementary, its well-known game design using blocks continues to insipire me with ideas. Instead of completely sticking with blocks, I combined the art work with other simple geometric shapes to add more life to this project. While the mountains in the background are made only using chunks of cubes, curved objects are used for designs like the rooftop.
